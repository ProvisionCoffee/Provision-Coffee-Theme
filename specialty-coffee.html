Specialty Coffee

We want to make enjoying coffee simple and easy so we categorize our coffees and identify them by the color of the wax label. Over time we seasonally rotate our Red, Black, and White Label coffee offerings for variety and freshness.

  • Red | Fruity & flavorful. Ideal for manual brewing and coffee purists.
  • Black | Chocolatey & rich. Ideal for espresso & auto-drip coffee makers.
  • White | Decaf. Just as delicious, but less caffeine.
