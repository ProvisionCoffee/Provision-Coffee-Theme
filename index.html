Empowering Farmers

​

Provision Coffee is committed to giving back to the various farmers who support us around the world. It is our belief that their community empowers ours and vise versa. By providing them a sustainable trade agreement and contributing directly to their education, health, and economic needs, they are in turn providing us with specialty coffees that are used to build community amongst our friends and family.

We help provide preventative care and emergency aid while promoting long term health by improving water quality and nutrition.
We work to provide education options for students and improving school facilities, equipment, and supplies.
We assist in improving coffee quality and increasing crop yields by investing in equipment and infrastructure and providing tools and micro-financing.
